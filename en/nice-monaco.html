The Nice-Monaco Route

The Corniche

Three legendary roads: the Lower, Middle and Upper Corniche. Each offers unique perspectives of the Mediterranean.

⏱️ Nice → Monaco: 30-45 min

🏛️

Èze Village

Perched at 427m altitude, this medieval village offers the most beautiful view of the French Riviera. An essential stop on your journey.

⏱️ Visit: 1h recommended

🎰

Monte-Carlo

Monaco's most prestigious district. Casino, palace, luxury yachts and extraordinary gardens await you.

⏱️ Visit: 2-3h minimum

Nice-Monaco Tour FAQ

What's the best route to Monaco?

The Middle Corniche offers the best compromise between spectacular views and travel time. Our drivers adapt the route according to your preferences and traffic.

How long should we spend in Monaco?

Minimum 2-3 hours for the essentials: Casino, Palace, Port. A full day allows you to fully enjoy with lunch and in-depth visits.

Does the tour include monument entries?

Transport and guide are included. Entries (Èze Exotic Garden, Prince's Palace, etc.) are extra. We can arrange tickets.

Can we stop for lunch?

Of course! Our team knows the best addresses: from panoramic terraces to gourmet restaurants. Reservation possible upon request.

Is the tour suitable for children?

Absolutely! We adapt the tour: less walking, shorter stops, Monaco's Oceanographic Museum is very popular with children.

Do you offer Nice-Monaco in the evening?

Yes! Monaco by night is magical. Casino, restaurants, lounge bars. Our team ensures safe return at any hour.

What languages is the tour available in?

Our team covers 5 languages: Hebrew, English, French, Spanish and Russian. A driver speaking your language will be assigned for the tour.

Can we combine with other destinations?

Yes! Nice-Monaco-Menton, or Nice-Èze-Cap Ferrat-Monaco. Our team creates custom tours according to your wishes.
