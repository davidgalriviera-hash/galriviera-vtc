Nice-Cannes Frequently Asked Questions

How do you get from Nice to Cannes?

The route goes along the coast through Antibes. Our drivers choose the best route based on traffic and weather, always with the most beautiful views.

How much time do you need to visit Cannes?

Minimum 2 hours for the essentials: La Croisette, Palais des Festivals. A full day allows you to enjoy beaches, museums and shopping peacefully.

Can you visit the Lérins Islands?

Yes! You can combine a visit to Sainte-Marguerite or Saint-Honorat islands. Our team arranges ferry tickets and plans perfect timing.

Where is the best place to shop?

La Croisette in Cannes for luxury boutiques, Rue d'Antibes for local shops. Our drivers know all the good addresses for every budget.

Is the route suitable for families?

Absolutely! Beaches, ice cream parlors, parks. We adapt the route: fewer museums, more beaches and activities for children.

Can you eat at kosher restaurants?

Our Israeli team knows all the kosher addresses in the area. We can arrange reservations and recommend the best places.

In which languages is the route available?

Our team covers 5 languages: Hebrew, English, French, Spanish and Russian. A driver speaking your language will be assigned to you for the route.

Can you add extra stops?

Of course! Grasse for perfumes, Renoir Museum, special beaches. Our team creates tailor-made routes according to your wishes.
