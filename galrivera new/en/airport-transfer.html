Transfer FAQ

How to book an airport transfer?

Contact us via WhatsApp with your flight details. Our team sends you a quote in 5 minutes and assigns a driver speaking your language.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Our team monitors your flight in real-time and automatically adjusts pickup time. No extra charge for flight delays.

Where do we meet the driver?

On arrival, your driver waits after customs with a sign with your name. For departures, pickup at your address.

Are child seats available?

Yes, baby seats and boosters available free on request. Specify children's age when booking.

How much luggage can I have?

Our standard vehicles accept 2 suitcases and 2 hand luggage per person. For more luggage, we adapt the vehicle.

Do you offer long-distance transfers?

Yes, our team ensures transfers to Paris, Lyon and all Europe. Multilingual service available for all long-distance trips.

How does the multilingual service work?

Our team covers 5 languages: Hebrew, English, French, Spanish and Russian. A driver speaking your language will be assigned according to your preferences.

What payment methods?

Payment at end of trip: credit card, cash, PayPal or transfer. No prepayment required.
